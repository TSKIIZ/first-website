FF Main Page

Free Fire is a battle royale game developed and published by Garena for Android and iOS. It became the most downloaded mobile game globally in 2019. As of 2023, Free Fire had surpassed 187 million daily active users.

Freefire Characters and their abilities

  1. Santino

    2. Santino was born in poverty, however, he made his way to the elite class by going to a renowned art school. He later established his fashion brand and is known for his fashion sense. The character comes with a special skill known as Shape Splitter, which spawns a mannequin forward and uses it to teleport to the mannequin’s position.

    Special sKill: Shape Splitter


  2. Luna

    3. Luna is a strong character who has matured early dealing with his father’s recklessness. She is a leader of a guild and is tasked to restore the order. Luna has a special ability known as Fight or Flight, which increases the firing rate. When the user hits an enemy, the firing rate will convert into a movement speed boost.

    Secial skill: Fight or Flight


  3. Tatsuya

    4. Tatsuya believes that he has the power to change the environment for the better. The character offers Rebel Rush a special ability that allows him to move forward at a rapid speed. The skill can be also used multiple times.

    Special ability: Rebel Rush


  4. Leon

    5. Leon is a university student and comes with extreme basketball skills. The character comes with Buzzer Beater survival ability that allows players to recover HP after engagement in combat.

    Special skill: HP Recovery


  5. Chrono

    6. Chorono has a special survival ability of Time Turner. It creates a force field that blocks damages from enemies and players can also fire from the inside as well. The force field also helps to increase movement speed during activation.
